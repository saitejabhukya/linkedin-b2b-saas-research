## Post 2 - My entire family worked corporate jobs. Justin Welsh

**Date:** 11th April 2025  
**Likes:** ~4300  
**Comments:** ~700  
**Post URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/justinwelsh_my-entire-family-worked-corporate-jobs-share-7448698455692066817-4mNL?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAAEL6NxkBB4npGCstcSOCaKRI5CDNKs1Jy2A

My entire family worked corporate jobs.

Salt of the earth people.
Midwestern hard workers.
Working for a steady paycheck.

Nobody is an entrepreneur.

There wasn't anyone "side hustling."

That just wasn't something that crossed their minds.

The path was simple:

Go to a good school, do well, land a good job, work there for 40+ years, and try your best to retire.

As you can imagine, when I left my startup CRO role to "build something on LinkedIn," the entire family thought that was madness.

I had landed that great job.
I'd worked the path like I was supposed to.

Why the hell would someone walk away from that?

And to be honest, I didn't really have a great answer other than the fact that I was burning the candle at both ends.

And I had burned out from the constant grind.

Nearly 7 years later, I've created my dream life.

And my business supports that life rather than being the centerpiece of it.

I've gained more freedom than anyone in my family ever had.

And it wasn't from anything special.

It was from asking, "Could this be done another way?"

Remember:

You don't need anyone's approval to do your thing.

You're allowed to be the first person to do it differently.

At some point, someone's gonna be first.
Why not you?

Never build someone else's idea of a great life. 
Build your own.
